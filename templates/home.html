Making the world a better place, one xray at a time

Blaze leverages the power of Artificial Intelligence to help healthcare workers diagnose and treat patients faster and more efficieantly. It can be used as a companion while diagnosing or could be used as a tool to train medical professionals.

How it works

The xray is analyzed by a pretrained convolutional neural network trained on a combination of these datasets: NIH, RSNA, CheXpert, Mimic-CXR and PadChest. The AI then runs the image through a neural network which predicts the probabilities of each disease. FAQ's

Nope, this project does not aim to completely replace doctors. It is just a mere companion to assist doctors in diagnosing and treating a patient.

The average human radiologist have error rates of upto 5%. With sufficient data supplied into the AI's dataset, it can theoretically reach and surpass a human's accuracy. Even if it does not reach such high accuracy levels, it can still be used to aid humans.
